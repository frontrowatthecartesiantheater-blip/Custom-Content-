About

A contractor who traded his toolbelt for a keyboard.

After two decades in residential construction and renovation, I understand the language of the industry—because I've lived it.

Now I combine that hands-on experience with professional writing to help construction businesses and industry publications stand out online and in print.

My style is clear, compelling, and grounded in real-world experience—shaped by years of business ownership, thorough research, and a deep respect for the power of well-chosen words.

Whether you need web copy, case studies, project spotlights, RFPs, white papers, or publication-ready articles, I help your organization communicate with authority and precision.
